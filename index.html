About This Project

Who are the artists in the JAM catalogue who were also part of Artists Space?

This viewer cross-references the Just Above Midtown: Changing Spaces exhibition catalogue (MoMA, 2022) with the "5000 Artists Return to Artists Space" publication.

Just Above Midtown (JAM) was a New York gallery founded by Linda Goode Bryant in 1974, dedicated to exhibiting work by African American artists and artists of color.

Artists who appear in both are highlighted in green throughout the catalogue. Click on any name to see all pages where they appear, highlighted in yellow.
